Visit Sidmouth

A UNESCO World Heritage Site situated along East Devon's Jurassic Coast.

Welcome to Sidmouth

Surrounded by beautiful countryside, with dramatic red stone cliffs and Blue Flag award winning beaches, Sidmouth is a wonderful place to stay. Explore the South West Coast Path, enjoy a cream tea or gelato, try some watersports or simply relax on the beach.

Connaught Gardens

The 'Jewel in the Crown' of East Devon, these gardens are situated high up on the cliff giving incredible views over the coastline and Sidmouth town.

Gelato

Sidmouth Donkey Sanctuary

Home to hundreds of donkeys this attraction has something special to offer. There's much to explore with activities, trails and talks, a maze, gift shop and award-winning restaurant.
